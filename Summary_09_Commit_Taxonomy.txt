What do large commits tell us? A taxonomical study of large commits

This Passage mainly told a taxonomy of commits by studying its purpose, and limited to larges projects (large commits).
Idea basically from an earlier study, they extend the categories. 
Furthermore, they dive into 9 specific project repository to establish an data summary of all commits and come out with the proportion of different types.
The conclusion is the large commits are more perfective than corrective.
